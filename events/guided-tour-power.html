Guided Tour: Exploring Alternative Energy

Date and Time: To be Announced

Embark on a fascinating guided tour in the 'Power of Tomorrow' exhibition at the Science Museum. Explore innovative solutions in alternative energy, sustainable practices, and the latest advancements in clean and renewable technologies.

Our expert guides will lead you through interactive exhibits, showcasing the power of solar, wind, and other alternative energy sources. Learn about the impact of these technologies on the environment and how they contribute to a sustainable future.
