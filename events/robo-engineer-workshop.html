Robo-Engineer Workshop at the Science Museum

Ignite Curiosity with the Robo-Engineer Workshop

Date and Time: To be Announced

Calling all young engineers! Join us for an immersive Robo-Engineer Workshop at the Science Museum, where kids can dive into the fascinating world of robotics. This hands-on event is designed to spark creativity and curiosity in budding scientists and engineers.

Participants will have the chance to engage in interactive activities, build their own robots, and explore the principles of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) education in a fun and exciting way. It's a day of learning, discovery, and the thrill of engineering!
