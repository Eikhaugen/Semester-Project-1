Cosmic Quest Adventure

February 18th from 11:00 to 14:00

Embark on an intergalactic journey!

Join us for an immersive space exploration experience where young astronauts can learn about planets, stars, and the mysteries of the universe through hands-on activities and interactive exhibits.

Dino Discovery Expedition

March 10th from 10:30 to 15:30

Unearth the world of dinosaurs!

Young paleontologists will love this prehistoric adventure, featuring lifelike dinosaur replicas, fossil digging activities, and a chance to travel back in time to discover the fascinating world of these ancient creatures.

Robo-Engineer Workshop

April 5th from 11:00 to 16:00

Build, code, innovate!

Dive into the world of robotics and technology. Kids will have a blast constructing their own robots, learning basic coding skills, and understanding the principles of engineering in this interactive and educational workshop.

Marine Marvels Aquarium

May 20th from 10:00 to 15:00

Dive into ocean wonders!

Explore the wonders of the deep blue sea with our marine-themed event. From touch tanks to interactive exhibits, children will discover the diversity of marine life, conservation efforts, and the importance of preserving our oceans.

Science Safari

June 15th from 11:30 to 15:00

Venture into the wild wonders of science!

Embark on a science-themed safari where young explorers can encounter a variety of interactive exhibits on biology, ecology, and the animal kingdom. This event combines learning with adventure for an unforgettable experience.

Mad Science Lab Extravaganza

July 18th from 11:30 to 14:30

Enter the realm of wacky experiments!

Calling all budding scientists! Join us in our Mad Science Lab for a day filled with entertaining and educational experiments. Kids will witness the magic of chemistry, physics, and biology in a fun and safe environment, sparking their curiosity and love for science.
