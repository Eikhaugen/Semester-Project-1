The new cosmology exhibition at the Science Museum

Discovering the Universe: Cosmology and Space Exploration Unveiled

Exhibition opens January 2nd, 2024

Welcome to the captivating world of cosmology! This exhibition takes you on a thrilling journey through the fundamental principles that govern the cosmos. From the formation of galaxies to the mysteries of dark matter, explore the science that propels us into the vast reaches of the universe.

Embark on an interactive exploration of the mechanics behind space travel. Engage with hands-on exhibits, simulations, and educational displays that unravel the mysteries of gravity, orbits, and the celestial ballet that shapes our understanding of the cosmos.

Join us as we celebrate the pioneers of space exploration and showcase the cutting-edge technologies that enable us to reach new heights. Whether you're a seasoned astronomer or a curious mind, this exhibition promises an immersive experience that will leave you inspired by the wonders of the universe.

Prepare for liftoff as we delve into the past, present, and future of space exploration, inviting you to witness the extraordinary feats of human ingenuity that propel us beyond the confines of our home planet.

A model display of iconic spacecraft used in space exploration.

From Pioneer Probes to Interstellar Voyages: A Legacy of Exploration

Trace the evolution of space exploration through iconic spacecraft that have ventured into the unknown. From the pioneering missions of early probes to the sophisticated interstellar voyages exploring distant star systems, witness the technological milestones that define our journey beyond Earth.

Engage with detailed models and interactive exhibits that highlight the challenges and triumphs of each mission. Explore the role of cosmology in guiding spacecraft through the cosmos and learn how these missions have expanded our understanding of the universe and beyond.

This section of the exhibition pays tribute to the visionaries who dared to explore the cosmos and the engineers who turned those dreams into reality. It's a testament to human curiosity and the relentless pursuit of knowledge that drives us to explore the frontiers of space.

Join us on this cosmic odyssey as we celebrate the achievements that have shaped the history of space exploration.
